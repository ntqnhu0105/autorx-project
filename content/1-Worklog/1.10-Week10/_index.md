### Week 10 Objectives:

* Learn the process of modernizing applications by migrating from a monolithic architecture to microservices.
* Gain hands-on experience in building and deploying microservice components.

### Week 10 Achievements:

* Gained a solid understanding of application modernization by migrating from a monolithic architecture to microservices.

* Acquired the ability to test, update, and interact with application APIs in a local development environment using Eclipse IDE.

* Learned the core principles of microservices design, including:
  * Service decomposition.
  * Data restructuring.
  * Event-Driven Architecture.
  * Microservice Messaging.

* Became familiar with building and authenticating Single Page Applications (SPAs).

* Developed a broad understanding of AWS AI services and their practical applications in modern software development.

* Learned how to orchestrate business processes using AWS Step Functions through State Machines and workflow automation.

* Successfully deployed serverless applications using:
  * AWS Lambda.
  * Amazon S3.
  * Amazon DynamoDB.

* Understood the Serverless Computing model and its benefits for building scalable, flexible, and cost-efficient applications.

* Expanded AWS knowledge and kept up with emerging cloud technologies by participating in the FCAJ Community Day Online.
