#### Create the SmartVehicle_Vehicles Table

From the **AWS Management Console** search bar, search for **DynamoDB** and navigate to **Tables**. Then choose **Create table**.

![table](/images/5-Workshop/5.3-DynamoDB/5.3.1-Vehicles/smartvehicle_vehicles.jpg)

Keep the default settings and select **On-demand** as the **Capacity mode**.

Then choose **Create table**.

![table](/images/5-Workshop/5.3-DynamoDB/5.3.1-Vehicles/smartvehicle_vehicles_1.jpg)

After a few seconds, the table status will change to **Active**.
