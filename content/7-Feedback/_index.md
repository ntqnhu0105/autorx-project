### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
What impressed me the most was the friendly and open working environment. Everyone on the team was always willing to help whenever I encountered difficulties, even with small issues. As an intern, I never felt pressured when asking questions or discussing problems with my mentors and teammates. This made me more confident throughout my learning and working experience.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor closely followed my internship progress, provided clear guidance, and patiently explained concepts whenever I had difficulties. Instead of giving me the answers directly, my mentor often guided me toward finding the solutions on my own, which helped me improve my problem-solving skills and independent thinking. The team admin was also very responsive and supportive whenever I needed information or assistance with administrative matters.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
Although the internship focused more on Cloud technologies than Backend development, I found it closely related to my Software Engineering background. The experience helped me better understand how backend systems are deployed, operated, and integrated on cloud platforms. I also had the opportunity to apply my knowledge of programming, APIs, and databases through practical tasks, which broadened my understanding of the software development and deployment process in real-world projects.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
This internship helped me improve various skills, including teamwork, time management, researching technical documentation, and professional communication in a workplace environment. Besides technical knowledge, I also learned how to approach problems systematically and collaborate effectively with team members to complete tasks.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
I really appreciated the collaborative spirit within the team. Everyone was willing to share their knowledge and provide constructive feedback. The working atmosphere was professional yet comfortable, making it easy for me to adapt and feel like part of the team, even as an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
I highly appreciated the opportunities to participate in Meetup sessions and listen to the experiences shared by professionals from different companies. The flexible working schedule also made it easier for me to balance my studies and internship responsibilities.

### Additional Questions
- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
Yes, I would. I believe this is a great environment for learning and personal growth, especially for students with limited industry experience. The mentors are supportive, the teammates are friendly, and interns are given opportunities to work on meaningful tasks instead of only handling simple or repetitive work.
