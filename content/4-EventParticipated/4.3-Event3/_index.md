# FCAJ Meetup - Event Report

### Event Objectives

- Share learning experiences and personal development journeys from students within the FCAJ community.
- Spread practical knowledge about AWS, AI, DevOps, and software development through real-world projects and experiences.
- Inspire participants to build products, engage in communities, and join technology competitions to develop their skills and careers.
- Connect students who are passionate about technology and create an environment for learning, sharing, and growth within the FCAJ community.

### Key Highlights

#### Learning AWS Effectively with Cloud Quest and Floci

- Addressed common challenges faced by AWS beginners, including **cost management**, **resource management**, and concerns about unexpected charges.
- Introduced **AWS Cloud Quest**, a gamified platform for learning AWS through hands-on missions.
- Explored **Floci**, an open-source tool that simulates AWS services for local development and testing.
- Presented a learning pathway: **Cloud Quest → Floci → Real AWS Environment**.

#### Overcoming Procrastination (The Iceberg of Procrastination)

- Explained that procrastination is often rooted in **fear**, rather than laziness.
- Introduced the **Iceberg Model** to identify hidden layers of fear.
- Shared practical techniques such as the **5-Minute Rule** and the mindset of **Action > Confidence**.
- Inspired participants through real-life stories and the philosophy of becoming an **AWS Builder**.

#### Building Confidence

- Explored the true meaning of **confidence** in learning and professional growth.
- Discussed psychological concepts such as **Imposter Syndrome** and the **Dunning-Kruger Effect**.
- Shared strategies for building confidence through small, consistent actions.
- Emphasized the importance of soft skills alongside technical expertise.

#### Hackathon – Learning Through Real Experience

- Introduced the purpose and value of participating in Hackathons.
- Shared experiences of building products under strict time constraints.
- Highlighted key lessons in teamwork, persistence, and problem-solving.
- Encouraged students to join Hackathons to accelerate skill development.

#### DevOps Foundation Mindset

- Analyzed the **Project Iceberg Model** and the root causes behind system failures and project challenges.
- Introduced the **DevOps mindset** through three key elements: **People – Process – Technology**.
- Discussed the importance of **Collaboration**, **Automation**, **Fast Feedback**, and **Continuous Improvement**.
- Encouraged a shift from reactive problem-solving to proactive prevention and continuous enhancement.
