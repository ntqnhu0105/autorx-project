# FCAJ Community Day - Event Report

### Event Objectives

- Share knowledge about Artificial Intelligence (AI), Cloud Computing, and emerging technologies on AWS.
- Introduce real-world applications and deployment architectures through live demonstrations.
- Inspire participants to build innovative products and pursue careers in the technology industry.
- Bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical experience shared by industry experts and the technology community.

### Key Highlights

#### AI Works Better with Context

- The importance of **context** in improving AI response quality.
- The transition from **prompt engineering** to **memory-enabled AI** (Second AI Brain).
- Learning and career development guidance for students interested in AI.

#### Building an AI Assistant with Amazon Quick

- Exploring **Quick Chat, Quick Flows, Quick Spaces, and QuickSight**.
- Creating workflows and dashboards using **natural language**.
- Supporting enterprise data analysis and collaboration with AI.

#### Amazon CloudFront for Every Workload

- Optimizing **cost**, **performance**, and **reliability**.
- Enhancing application **security**.
- Understanding the role of CloudFront in modern cloud architectures.

#### Building a Real-World Product

- Sharing the development journey of **UTMorpho** at LotusHacks.
- Experience building a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) in just **36 hours**.
- Lessons learned throughout the product development process.

#### Multi-Agent Systems for Enterprise Applications

- Comparing **Single-Agent** and **Multi-Agent** architectures.
- Applying Multi-Agent systems to credit scoring solutions.
- Designing AI systems with proper **guardrails** and **compliance**.

- **Context-first approach:** Context is more important than simply optimizing prompts.
- AI systems can leverage memory and contextual information to produce more relevant and accurate results.
- Gained a clearer understanding of the learning roadmap and career path in AI.

#### AWS Architecture & Services

- Learned how to build AI assistants using **Amazon Quick**.
- Understood how **Amazon CloudFront** improves global content delivery.
- Explored AI-powered data analysis and workflow automation.

#### Practical Experience

- Learned the process of transforming ideas into real products.
- Understood how to build an MVP within a short timeframe.
- Gained insights into teamwork and problem-solving during product development.

#### Advanced AI Concepts

- Understood the limitations of deterministic behavior in Large Language Models (LLMs).
- Learned when to use **Single-Agent** or **Multi-Agent** architectures.
- Gained insights into designing AI systems that are both effective and secure.
